Privacy Policy

Last Updated: February 2026

1. Information Collection

At Shine & Bright, we collect personal information necessary to provide our laundry services, including:

  • Contact details (Name, Phone Number, Email).
  • Delivery details (Physical Address for Pickups).
  • Technical data (IP addresses and Cookies for website performance).

2. Use of Information

We use your data strictly for:

  • Scheduling and completing laundry pickups/deliveries.
  • Sending order status updates and notifications.
  • Complying with legal obligations under Indian law.

3. Data Protection Rights

Under the DPDP Act 2023, you have the right to:

  • Request access to the personal data we hold about you.
  • Correct or update inaccurate information.
  • Request the deletion of your data once the service is fulfilled.
