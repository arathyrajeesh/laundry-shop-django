üë§ Account Issues

How do I reset my password? +

To reset your password:

  1. Go to Settings & Privacy
  2. Click "Change Password" under Account Preferences
  3. Enter your current password
  4. Enter your new password twice
  5. Click "Change Password"

You'll receive a confirmation email after successfully changing your password.

How do I update my profile information? +

To update your profile:

  1. Click on your profile picture in the top-right corner
  2. Select "View Profile"
  3. Click "Edit Profile"
  4. Update your information and save changes
How do I delete my account? +

Account deletion is permanent and cannot be undone. To delete your account:

  1. Go to Settings & Privacy
  2. Click "Delete Account" under Privacy
  3. Read the warnings carefully
  4. Check the confirmation box
  5. Click "Yes, Delete My Account"

You'll receive a confirmation email after your account is deleted.
